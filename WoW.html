What is World of Warcraft?

World of Warcraft (WoW) is an Massively Multiplayer Online Role-playing Game (MMORPG). World of Warcraft is developed by Blizzard Entertainment. It takes place in a fantasy world called "Azeroth" in which players take part of a neverending fight between two factions: Horde and Alliance. World of Warcraft is one of the most played MMORPGS in the world. It has apporiximetly 5 million concurrent subscribers. World of Warcraft was released in 2004 and there has been released 7 expansions during its lifetime.

Why you should play WoW?

World of Warcraft is one of the most iconic video games ever released. World of Warcraft connects people and makes you feel like you are part of something. World of Warcraft also has alot of content which in which you can take part of with your friends of by yourself. With new content releasing in fairly regular basis, the content never ends.

How you can play WoW?

First you have to go to Battle.net (one of the links) and then you must make an account. After that, you buy subsctiption which ranges from 1 month to a year. After subscription, download the client from Battle.net, download the game from the client and you are good to go.
